Hivebrew

Hivebrew is a third person game. It puts players in the role of an alchemist combatting giant insects. The game loop consists of collecting resources in order to craft potions and use them to defeat the insects roaming the map.
Key Features: Character movement, strategic combat, dynamic AI, navigation mesh, inventory system.
